# User Interviews

## ICP Definition

### Primary: Startup CTO / VP Engineering
- Team size: 10-50 engineers
- AI tool spend: $2K-20K/month
- Pain: "I know we're overspending but I don't have time to audit it"
- Behavior: Approves tool purchases reactively, rarely reviews

### Secondary: Engineering Manager
- Manages team of 5-15
- Owns tool budget for their team
- Pain: "We have 3 AI coding assistants and I don't know which we actually use"
- Behavior: Gets pinged about budget quarterly, scrambles to justify spend

### Tertiary: Indie Hacker / Solo Founder
- 1-3 person team
- AI tool spend: $100-500/month
- Pain: "Am I on the right plan? Is there something cheaper?"
- Behavior: Price-sensitive, willing to switch tools for savings

## Interview Questions (Planned)

### Discovery
1. How many AI tools does your team use? Can you list them?
2. What's your approximate monthly spend on AI/ML tooling?
3. When was the last time you reviewed your AI tool subscriptions?
4. Have you ever discovered you were paying for unused seats?
5. How do you decide which plan tier to use for a tool?

### Value Validation
6. If a tool could show you exactly where you're overspending on AI tools in 60 seconds, would you use it?
7. Would you share those results with your co-founder or CFO?
8. What would make you trust the recommendations?
9. Would you provide your email to get a PDF report of the analysis?
