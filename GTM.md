# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Positioning

**One-liner:** "Find wasted AI spend before your next invoice."

**Category:** B2B SaaS — AI spend optimization / lead generation

**Positioning statement:** For startup engineering teams who overspend on AI tooling, StackTrim is a free audit tool that identifies concrete savings opportunities using deterministic analysis of their current subscriptions, unlike manual spreadsheet reviews or expensive procurement consultants.

## Distribution Strategy

### Phase 1: Product-Led (Current)
- Free audit with no signup required
- Value delivered before email capture
- Optimized for screenshot sharing (results page designed to be shareable)
- Public share URLs with OpenGraph previews (future)

### Phase 2: Content-Led
- "AI Spend Benchmarks" report (anonymized, aggregated audit data)
- Blog posts: "How much should a 20-person startup spend on AI tools?"
- Twitter/LinkedIn distribution targeting startup CTOs

### Phase 3: Credex Integration
- Audit results include Credex credit procurement opportunities
- Warm leads passed to Credex sales team
- Co-branded audit reports
